Expectation

It is impossible for an individual to even muster a desire to think about giving any effort towards achieving a goal unless hope and belief exist within them that it is possible to achieve that goal. Therefore, a vital step in achieving any goal is to nurture hope and belief within yourself that accomplishing your goals is possible. The question is, how do you do that?

Evaluation

A key to developing and growing a personal hope and belief that achieving your goals is possible is to recognize and celebrate your personal efforts and achievements, regardless of the immediate outcome. This could even mean recognizing an effort made that didn’t get the result you wanted. The fact that you tried something and it failed, in the very least means that you learned something. If all you learned is that something doesn’t work, that is a step in the right direction and progress that wasn’t there before. Celebrating your personal successes will help you to focus on and recognize your progress, instead of focusing on what hasn’t happened yet. Eventually those triumphs will yield not only the end result you are striving for, but also the wisdom, character, and ability to produce results like that again and again. Most importantly, celebrating your successes, however small or big, will nurture hope and belief that your goals are possible.
