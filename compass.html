Perspective

Our motivation to act on our goals becomes deflated when we convince ourselves that accomplishing our goal isnâ€™t all that important. Belittling our goals can lead to losing perspective and seeking immediate gratification. Immediate gratification means we are neglecting the big picture to get something we want right now. This short sightedness leads to spending time in frivolous, destructive, or ultimately unfulfilling ways. These actions lead to discouragement. How do we avoid this short sightedness and keep our desires and goals in line with each other?

Purpose

The importance of a goal remains clear to us when we know how that goal ties into our purpose. Our purpose defines what is important to us in life. We can cultivate our immediate desires by knowing our ultimate goals or purpose in life. Purpose gives our goals, actions, efforts, and work meaning. This meaning is a strong motivator and fuel for action. Fulfillment comes from accomplishing things that line up with our purpose. Knowing our purpose will form a personal compass that will guide the decisions we make day to day and help us to define what our personal success will look like and mean.

Paradigm

Another important step in nurturing hope and belief that achieving your goals is possible is to fill your mind with positive things and develop optimism. This site will provide you with resources for you to collect positive quotes and ideas from books, articles, and other writings or resources. An important step in achieving your goals is to keep your hope and belief alive and growing each day. Establishing the habit of reading and reviewing positive quotes, principles, or ideas daily in some way is a necessary mental exercise for maintaining belief and hope. Personal hope and optimism are a mindset that needs to be replenished each day. This is an important step in reaching any goal.
